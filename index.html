When AI becomes the informal research assistant, reference librarian, and study partner, what happens when it invents, misquotes, and erases marginalized intellectual histories?
The term “hallucination” serves to rebrand and mystify mistakes. It turns technical errors into an opportunity to sell LLMs as more humanlike than they really are.

Thus the risk is that people disseminate text generated by LLMs, meaning more text in the world that reinforces and propagates stereotypes and problematic associations, both to humans who encounter the text and to future LLMs trained on training sets that ingested the previous generation LLM’s output.” source: On the Dangers of the Stochastic Parrot
